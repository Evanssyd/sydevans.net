Ispikit SDKs

Ispikit speech SDK are a set of speech libraries built on top of CMU pocketsphinx. These libraries are built in C++ and ported to a number of platforms. In the most general terms Ispikit takes any recorded audio and output how well you said that phrase. With word level scoring and mispornunciation spotting.

Check it out!
